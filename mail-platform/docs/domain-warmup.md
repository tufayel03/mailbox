# Domain Warmup

## Goal

Protect new IP/domain reputation and improve Gmail inbox placement.

## Recommended warmup (per new mailbox/domain)

- Day 1: up to 20 messages
- Day 2: up to 30
- Day 3: up to 45
- Day 4: up to 65
- Day 5: up to 90
- Day 6: up to 120
- Day 7: up to 150
- Day 8+: hold near 200/day cap

## Rules

- Keep complaint and bounce rates very low.
- Send to engaged/known recipients first.
- Maintain SPF, DKIM, DMARC alignment.
- Do not blast cold lists on day 1.
